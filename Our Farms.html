Our Farms

GreenHive Farms is committed to sustainable urban agriculture through innovative farming technologies. We use hydroponics, vertical farming, and smart monitoring systems to grow fresh produce while minimizing environmental impact.

Our Farming Technologies

Hydroponic Farming

Plants are grown in nutrient-rich water instead of soil, allowing faster growth and efficient water usage.

Vertical Farming

Crops are cultivated in stacked layers to maximize space and improve productivity in urban areas.

Smart Sensors

Environmental sensors monitor temperature, humidity and nutrients to maintain ideal growing conditions.
