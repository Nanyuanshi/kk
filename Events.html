Activities & Events

Discover the exciting activities and events happening at GreenHive Farms. Join us to learn, explore, and enjoy sustainable urban farming experiences.

Regular Activities

Weekly Harvesting Sessions

Fun and easy sessions for kids to learn about plants and how to grow food in the city.

Community Gardening Days

Join fellow community members every first Sunday of the month to help maintain the farm and share gardening tips.

Seasonal Planting Workshops

Learn how to plant different crops each season with help from our expert farmers.

Sensor Setup Training

Learn how to set up and use smart sensors to monitor plant health and growth.

Hydroponics Basics Class

A simple class to teach how to grow plants without soil using water and nutrients.

Farm Volunteer Program

Volunteer regularly to help with daily farm tasks and learn about urban farming hands-on.
