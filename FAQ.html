Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find simple answers here or contact us if you need more help.

General & Mission

What is GreenHive Farms?

GreenHive Farms is a city-based farm using technology to grow fresh vegetables and herbs in a clean, eco-friendly way.

What is your mission?

We want to provide fresh, healthy food to our community while protecting the environment by using smart farming methods.

What does "urban farming" mean?

It means growing food inside the city using space-saving methods like vertical farming and hydroponics.

Technology & Growing Methods

What is hydroponics?

Hydroponics is growing plants without soil, using water mixed with nutrients to feed the plants directly.

How does vertical farming work?

We grow plants in stacked layers to save space and grow more food in a small area.

What is smart sensor technology?

We use sensors to check and control water, light, and nutrients to help plants grow their best.

Do you use pesticides or chemicals?

No. Our indoor farm is clean and controlled, so we don’t need harmful chemicals.

Are your vegetables organic?

Our plants are grown naturally without pesticides, but hydroponic farms have different organic rules.

How do you save water?

We recycle water in our system, using up to 90% less water than regular farms.

Products & Availability

What do you grow?

We grow leafy greens like lettuce and spinach, herbs like basil and cilantro, and fresh microgreens.

Where can I buy your produce?

You can buy from our farm store or order online for local delivery soon.

Do you supply restaurants?

Yes, we work with local chefs to provide fresh ingredients daily.

How long do your vegetables stay fresh?

Because we harvest right before delivery and grow without soil, our produce stays fresh longer than store-bought.

Tours & Community Events

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes, we have guided tours for individuals, schools, and groups to see how we farm with technology.

How do I book a tour?

You can book your visit on our website’s booking page soon.

Are workshops good for kids?

Yes, we have fun and easy workshops designed for children to learn about plants and farming.

What is a farm-to-table experience?

You pick fresh veggies and enjoy meals made with them, all at our farm.

Visiting Us

Where is GreenHive Farms?

We are located in the city center. Check our Contact page for the address and hours.

When is the farm open?

We are open Tuesday to Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM. Tours need to be booked in advance.
